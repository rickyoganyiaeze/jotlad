About Jotlad Group of Schools

Jotlad Group of Schools is a distinguished educational institution comprising Jotlad Nursery & Basic School and Jotlad Model College. Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing quality education that nurtures the intellectual, moral, and social development of every child.

Our dedicated team of educators uses innovative teaching methods to ensure each student reaches their full potential in a supportive and stimulating environment.
